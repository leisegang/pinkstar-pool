FAQ

  • Where are my stats?

    They were lost during the FORK of the PNKS to PSTAR, payment was done before reset. Stats are lost but we continue to mine here at poolmining.us

  • Can i use Worker at this pool?

    YES, you can use wallet_id+workername at this pool. Use walletID+workerID if you want fixed diff use: walletID+workerID.fixeddiff -> VqKbKuRE+PC.50000 to set worker name to PC and fixed diff to 50000.

  • Can i use fixed difficulty at this pool?

    For fixed diff use wallet.diff -> wallet_id.100000 You can calculate optimal difficulty as: WORKER HASHRATE x 60 SECONDS. So if average hashrate of your miner connection is 100 H/S, than optimal fixed difficulty will be 6000.

  • Is the hasrate on dashboard accurate?

    Yes and no! The Hashrate in your miner is the actual hashrate. The dashboard shows effective hashrate. If you solve a 10000 share after 5000 you get higher effective hashrate. If you solve it at 13000 its lower than what your miner shows.

  • Where is the pool/server located?

    Server is in France. Domain is (.us) but its located in france.

  • Can i mine directy to Cryptopia / exchange?

    NO! You can not mine directly to exchange wallet, your coins will be lost!

  • What are orphaned blocks?

    Detached or Orphaned blocks are valid blocks which are not part of the main chain. They can occur naturally when two miners produce blocks at similar times. Read more here: StackExchange.
